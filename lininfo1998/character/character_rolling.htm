Character Rolling
Since STR, DEX, CON, INT, WIS and CHR are fixed after created character, and the state are randomly made, so it is important to roll a good character. Each job of character have different focus on states. For example, Knights are good at STR. But anyway the sum of the 6 states of each job are always 75.

We should roll a good STR, DEX and CON character because INT, WIS and CHR are unless currently.

The rolling method is very simple, we can roll character by clicking on the dices in create char. menu many times.

Sometime, if we are lucky, we could get 18/01, 18/02 or 18/03 STR character. 18/01 means 19 STR, and 18/02 means 20 STR and so on. Lineage use 18/01 instead of 19 because the value 18 is the limit of the state in RPG. However, all the state other than STR will not get 18/01 or higher.

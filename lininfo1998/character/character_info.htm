Character Info

Right now, there are 5 types of Character in Lineage. They're prince, princess, male knight, female knight and elf. However, the properties of prince and princess, or males knight and female knight is the same. So in fact there is only 3 types of character.

Since all characters can wear every armor and weapon in Lineage, the only difference the state. State inclued Strength(STR), Dexterity(DEX), Constitution(CON), Intelligence(INT), Wisdom(WIS), and Charisma(CHR), Level(LEV) and Armor Class(AC). Level can be raise by killing monsters, Armor Class can be change by wearing armor(lower AC is better). However, STR, DEX, CON, INT, WIS and CHR are fixed after create character, they will not change when Level raise. The table below is the function of each state:

State Function
State Short Form Function
Level LEV
  • Hit roll (probability of hit success)
Armor Class AC
  • Determines the chance of being hit.
  • Reduces damage
    (Lower AC is better)
Strength STR
  • Hit roll (probability of hit success)
  • Damage roll (amount of damage to enemy except for missle weapons (bow and arrows))
  • Amount of your carrying capacity.
Dexterity DEX
  • Hit roll (probability of hit success)
Constitution CON
  • Amount of max HP gained when you level up
  • HP regeneration speed
Intelligence INT
  • Currently useless
Wisdom WIS
  • Currently useless
Charisma CHR
  • Currently useless

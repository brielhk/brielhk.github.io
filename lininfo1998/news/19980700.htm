12:15 HKT - How do use 1 ID scroll to identiy mutil-items?
I have discovered this method for long, and today I proved it is true. First of all, before I tell the method, please see one of my items: (I have not edited this picture)

If you are smart, you will know this T-shirt is identified because it showed it is blessed.
If you are even smarter, you will find error in this picture. How come a identified armor without enchant value?!~ It is because I am using the method. ^_^

In order to be successed by using this method, you should remind that:

  • Your connection to Lineage should not be too fast.
  • You should have a good mouse pointing and clicking skill.
  • By using this method, only the first item that you identified will show its enchant value. Other items that you identified by using this method will only show if it is normal, blessed or cursed.
  • By using this method, only the first item that you identified will stay identified after restart. Other items that you identified by using this method will show as unidentified.
  • I will not responsible for any loss of items by using this method.

OK, let me tell you about this method... :-D

  • Firstly, put your scroll of identify into the bottom of your inventory
  • Secondly, put the items that you want to identify near the scroll of identify in order to reduce the time by moving between the scroll and the items. (But don't put any of the items to the bottom)
  • Thirdly, wait until your feel you are lagging.
  • The last step, double click on the scroll of identify, point and click on the item you want to identify. Then double click on the scroll of identify again (the scroll of identify didn't disappear since you are lag), and point and click on the item you want to identify again... Repeat these action again and again, then you would get many identify items without enchant value... enjoy them! ^o^ (It is best to identify scroll of enchant weapon or armor. ^o^)

Once again, don't blame me if you fail or loss item(s) by this method...

Today Lineage didn't update, I got nothing to do. So I go to found out the "life" of some items. A new candle will last for 10 minutes(600 seconds), and a new lamp will last for 50 minutes(3000 seconds). Therefore 1 point of fuel of the lighting devices will last for 1 seconds. (a new candle has 600 fuel, and a new lamp has 3000 fuel)

Then I go to test when will the effect of opaque potion and green potion last. A normal(not blessed nor cursed) opaque potion will blind us for 7.5 minutes(450 seconds), and a normal green potion will haste us for 3.33 minutes(200 seconds). Therefore we can said there is "450 fuel" in normal opaque potion, and "200 fuel" in normal green potion.

Storage

Dorin
Karim
Dorin and Karim will help Level 5 or above players to store their items. Each player's account have 50 items space to store their items and each pledge have 50 items space as well. We have to pay 20 adena for each items we want to take out from the storage. Dorin is located on Island's town while Karim is located on Main Land's town.
 

Quest
Gunter
Princes/Princesses and Knights can ask Gunter for a quest after level 15. Gunter is located in Gunter's House which is on the north-west from the Island's Town.
 

Kent Castle
Gayle
Gayle will repair our damaged weapon, he will charge 200 adena per erosion level. He is located inside Kent Castle Area.		 Hunt
Hunt will give us the bodyguards (blue sharks) to the bloodpledge's member after 1 day the king/queen have arranged the bodyguards  to members.
Count Guard & Guard (Archer)
Count guard and guard (archer) will protect the castle and the king/queen's pledge members. They are strong and will attack the invaders.
Gatekeeper
Gatekeeper will help the king/queen's bloodpledge to open or close the gate.

Bahof
Bahof, like Dorin or Karim, but will only help king/queen's bloodpledge to store or retrieve items.		 Ishmael
Ishmael will help the king/queen of Kent Castle manage the castle.

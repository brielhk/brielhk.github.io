Character Info
Right now, there are 6 different characters in Lineage. They're prince, princess, male knight, female knight and male elf and female elf. The properties between of the two gender is the same, therefore are only 3 types of character. Personally I suggest you play Knight classes because it is the easiest to control. And don't play prince/princess race unless you got many friends in Lineage and want to make a bloodpledge.
Picture Class Description
Prince They can create Bloodpledge and become the leader of it while other class cannot. They can wear most of the weapon and armor but they are not good at magic.
Princess
Knight Knights are good at fighting and they can wear most weapon and armor. However they are very bad at magic.
Elf Elves have average states, they are not as good as knight on fighting, but they can use magic well. They have night vision at night while other classes do not.
States' Function
State Short Form Function
Level LV
  • Hit roll (probability of hit success)
Armor Class AC
  • Determines the chance of being hit.
  • Reduces damage (Lower AC is better)
Strength STR
  • Hit roll (probability of hit success)
  • Damage roll (amount of damage to enemy except for missle weapons)
  • Amount of your carrying capacity.
Dexterity DEX
  • Hit roll (probability of hit success)
Constitution CON
  • Amount of max HP gained when you level up
  • HP regeneration speed
  • Amount of your carrying capacity
Intelligence INT
  • Spell strength. (currently useless)
Wisdom WIS
  • Magic resistance
Charisma CHR
  • Prince/Princess's CHR x 2 is the maximum number of members of their pledge.
  • Maximum number of bodyguard (CHR / 2)
  • Maximum number of monster we can train

Character Rolling
Since STR, DEX, CON, INT, WIS and CHR are not easy to change after created character, and the state are randomly made, so it is important to roll a good character. Each class of character have different focus on states. For example, Knights are good at STR. The sum of the 6 states of each class are 75 when we created character.

Currently, I suggest you to roll a good STR, DEX or CON characters, it is because other states is not as useful as them.

Sometime, if we are lucky, we could get 18/01, 18/02 or 18/03 STR character. 18/01 means 19 STR, and 18/02 means 20 STR and so on. Lineage use 18/01 instead of 19 because the value 18 is the limit of the state in AD&D games. However, all the state other than STR will not get 18/01 or higher.

The table below showed the minimum state of each class. The points left will disturbuted to the states randomly when we are rolling.  (They are come from the experiment of rolling 10000+ times)

Class Suggestion
Prince/Princess 1. Prince/Princess should roll high CHR because they can accept more members in order to create a bigger bloodpledge. 18 CHR is quite easy to get.

Monster List

Formula of experience of monsters:
- Experience = level x level + 1
- If monster's level is 9 or higher, add bonus 50.
- If monster's AC is 0, 1 or 2, add bonus: (7 - ac).
- If monster's AC is below 0, add bonus: (7 - ac) x 2.

Type:
I - aggressive (will not attack us unless we attack them)
II - aggressive (will attack us without reason)
III- aggressive in dungeon, passive on land
IV- aggressive on mainland, passive on island
V - aggressive to unmorphed players, passive to morphed players
VI - agressive to chaotic player, passive to others (neutral and lawful)
0 - none of the above type
